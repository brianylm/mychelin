## Plan B: If No Charity Partner by April 15

2. **Apply for charity registration** — takes 2-3 months but can start the process
3. **Focus on NHB Heritage Research Grant** — next cycle opens **July 2026**, needs institutional partner (could partner with NTU/NUS instead of charity)
4. **Self-fund the beta** — workshops at own cost, build impact metrics for future grant cycles

The HRG is actually a stronger fit for the AI language bridge R&D ($75-150K), and the July timeline gives more room. CMF is the fast-track opportunity.
