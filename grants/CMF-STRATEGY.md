### Recommended: Partner with Singapore Heritage Society (SHS)

**Why SHS:**
- Founded 1987, registered charity with **IPC status**
- Mission: preservation and documentation of Singapore's heritage
- Already engaged in food heritage discourse
- Would benefit from a tech platform to scale their mission
- Mychelin aligns perfectly with their charter

**Approach:**
1. Email SHS (contact via shs.org.sg) with a 1-page pitch
2. Propose: Mychelin operates under SHS as a digital heritage project
3. SHS receives the donations (from us, crowdfunding, corporate sponsors)
4. CMF matches those donations dollar-for-dollar
5. Matched funds pay for Mychelin development, workshops, community outreach

**Other potential charity partners:**

- **Peranakan Association Singapore** (strong heritage focus, narrower community)
- **Chinese Heritage Centre** (NTU-based, academic angle)
- **Malay Heritage Foundation** (for Malay cuisine heritage specifically)

## How CMF Works

1. You (or donors) give **cash donations** to the partner charity
2. The charity applies to CMF for dollar-for-dollar matching
3. CMF matches up to the donation amount
4. First $300K of matching has **full flexibility** in use (operating costs, salaries, rent, development)
5. Above $300K must align to "culture sector priorities"

**Example scenario:**
- B raises $20,000 in donations (personal + crowdfunding + small corporate sponsors)
- SHS applies for CMF matching → receives $20,000 matching
- Total available: **$40,000** for Mychelin development + community programs
- This covers: 6 months development, 10 heritage workshops, marketing
